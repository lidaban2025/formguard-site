Frequently Asked Questions

How do I install FormGuard?

Open a Google Form, go to Extensions → Add-ons → Get add-ons, search for "FormGuard", and click Install. Grant the required permissions and you're ready to go.

Why can't FormGuard access my other forms?

By design. FormGuard uses the forms.currentonly scope, which means it can only access the specific form where you installed it. This is a privacy and security feature, not a bug.

My form didn't close at the exact time I set. Why?

FormGuard uses Google Apps Script time-driven triggers, which may have a delay of up to a few minutes. This is a limitation of Google's platform, not FormGuard. For critical deadlines, we recommend setting the close time a few minutes early.

How do I upgrade to Pro or Lifetime?

Open FormGuard in your Google Form, click the ⚙️ settings icon, and select "Upgrade". You'll be guided through the purchase process.

Can I get a refund?

Yes! We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for both Pro and Lifetime plans. Just email us at lidaban2025@gmail.com within 14 days of purchase.

How do I uninstall FormGuard?

Go to Extensions → Add-ons → Manage add-ons in your Google Form. Find FormGuard and click the three-dot menu → Remove. All settings associated with that form will be cleared.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. FormGuard stores all data within Google's own infrastructure (Properties Service). No data ever leaves Google's servers. Read our Privacy Policy for full details.

I found a bug. How do I report it?

Email us at lidaban2025@gmail.com with: (1) what you expected to happen, (2) what actually happened, and (3) steps to reproduce the issue. Screenshots help!
